Our Story

Crafted with
Passion

Est. 2018 · Mumbai, India

Where Artistry
Meets Elegance

Maison Luxe was born from a simple belief: that every woman deserves to carry something beautiful. Founded in the vibrant city of Mumbai, our journey began in a small atelier where skilled craftspeople worked tirelessly to create bags that would endure the test of time.

We source the finest full-grain leathers from established tanneries, work with artisans who have honed their craft over generations, and create pieces that tell stories of their own. Each bag is a dialogue between tradition and modernity — respectful of heritage, yet alive to the present.

Today, Maison Luxe serves thousands of women across India and beyond, and our commitment remains unchanged: to craft bags worthy of your most memorable moments.

— Priya Sharma, Founder

Craftsmanship First
